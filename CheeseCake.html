Instructions

Set out all ingredients onto the counter (trust me its easier)

Crush two weetabix into a jar
Add 100ml of milk or just enough to make your base
Get a mug and add 100Grams of greek style yogurt with your protein powder and mix
Make sure your mixture has no powdered lumps
Yer mixture is complete!
Spread mixture across the top of the weetabix base
Crush two biscoff biscuits and set in the fridge for 20/30 minutes (or eat it as is)

Recipe Checklist

  • Weetabix

  • Greek Style Yogurt

  • Protein power of your choice (i have used white chocolate)

  • Semi-Skimmed Milk

  • Biscoff Biscuits
