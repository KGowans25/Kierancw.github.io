Finished Scran

Instructions

Put one or two hash browns into the oven
Butterfly chicken and lightly fry in oil(5ml worth)
Cook for 3/4mins per side depending on size of chicken
Chop halloumi and fry once chicken is almost done
Shove a greek kebab bread into the oven to bake for 3 minutes
Serve with lettuce and onions and some mayo
